About us

We are passionate about animals and believe that every little animal deserves love, care, and a cozy home. At Amor Peludo, our mission is to connect dedicated pet owners with quality products that provide well-being and happiness to their pets.

From interactive toys to healthy foods, we carefully select each item to ensure your little pet receives the best. Additionally, we share valuable tips and useful information to help you care for your pet with love and responsibility.

Our team is made up of animal lovers who understand the importance of treating each little creature with the respect and attention they deserve. Join us on this journey of love and care for our four-legged friends!

How can you adopt?

Adopting a pet is an act of love that transforms lives, both yours and the animal's. At Amor Peludo, we encourage responsible adoption, ensuring that each little animal finds a home full of affection and responsibility. Here are the steps to adopt:

  • Choose Your New Friend: Browse our catalog of animals available for adoption. Each one has a detailed profile with information about their personality, special needs, and history.
  • Fill Out the Adoption Form: After finding the ideal pet, complete our adoption form. This step is essential to better understand your expectations and ensure that the animal will adapt well to your lifestyle.
  • Sign the Adoption Agreement: If everything goes well, you will sign an adoption agreement that formalizes your commitment to caring for the new family member.
  • Adjustment and Follow-Up: In the first few days, we will follow up to ensure that the adjustment is going smoothly. We are here to offer support and answer any questions you may have.
  • Adopting is an act of love and responsibility. We are here to help at every step of this journey. Join our community and transform the life of a little animal!

How can you help the animal cause?

At Amor Peludo, we believe that everyone can contribute to the well-being of animals in various ways. Here are some ways you can get involved and make a difference:

  • Adopt a Pet: The most direct way to help is by adopting one of our animals. Provide a loving and caring home for a pet in need.
  • Become a Foster Home: If you can't adopt permanently, consider being a foster home. Hosting an animal until it finds a permanent home is an invaluable help.
  • Make Donations: Your financial contribution helps cover the costs of food, veterinary care, spaying/neutering, and other needs of our animals. Every donation makes a difference!
  • Volunteer: Donating your time is an excellent way to help. We need volunteers to care for the animals, participate in events, and assist with adoption campaigns.
  • Spread the Word: Share our posts on social media, talk about our work with friends and family. Increasing visibility helps find more adopters and supporters.
  • Participate in Our Campaigns: Engage in awareness campaigns and fundraising events. Your active participation is essential for the success of these initiatives.
  • Every gesture counts and makes a difference in the lives of animals. Join us at Amor Peludo and help promote responsible adoption and animal well-being. Your support is crucial!
